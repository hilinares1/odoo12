Disount on Purchase Lines

Falinwa Limited

Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo does not give the possibility to manage discount on purchase order line.

Falinwa behavior : you can add a discount on the purchase order line.

Module + : to combine with the vendor pricelist discount and the intercompany extra field to fully use this feature integrated in Odoo.

Business case : when you work on catalog, usually, the supplier proposes you a standard catalog price and you negotiate only your discount. With this module, you can import the whole catalog pricing to your database with the proper supplier price and discount, whcih will strongly help your purchaser to manage their purchases and negotiate better discount.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation : 100% of the database.

How To Use This Module

Step 1
  • Go to Purchases app, Request for Quotation, create a RFQ and add the discount on Products tab
    • Step 2
  • On Reports Purchase Analysis, you can see the Purchase Analysis
    • Step 3
  • On Reports Average Quantity, it displays the Purchase Average Quantity
