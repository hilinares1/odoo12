Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo manages multicurrencies but not multicurrencies reports (Opportunity, Sales order, P&L,...). For the CRM, Odoo propose to put opportunity in local currencies but when you logged at the parent company, it does not convert the opportunity amount to the group currency.

Falinwa behavior : we add the notion of Opportunity expected amount in Group currency in order to be able to make report for each entity or for the group, both on the opportunity amount and on the expected amount.

Module + : a must if you have a multi-company CRM with different company currencies.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation: when you have a multicompany databases with different currencies.
  • Recommendation / Best practice: the module call the amount in company currency only on the Kanban view by default. When you are on other view (piivot,..), you can search for this field (in measure for instance).
