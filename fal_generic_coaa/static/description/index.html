Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo does not propose a generic chart of account and let it fully open.

Falinwa behavior : based on Falinwa CFO experience, Falinwa generic CoAA proposes a general architecture of your analytic account tree in order to help you to define the global structure.

Module + : your analytic account structure is a key topic because it will define the whole controlling strategy of your company. This module is a good departure point to have something robust and used on different international companies.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation : if you do not have an internal chart of analytic account or if you want to change your current one.
  • Recommendation / Best practice: in addition of the generic CoAA, Falinwa recommends to have some consulting hours in order to challenge this CoAA against your internal needs and propose the best solution for your company. The chart of analytic account has to be the image of your company and so need to be Taylor-made for your activities and your organization.

The features

Default Analytic Account

To Analize details of your financial activity, one of features in odoo that very usefull is analytic account.

This falinwa standard module, consist of standard analytic account that ready to use.

How to use it ?

Go to menu :

Accounting > Configuration > Analytic Account > Analytic Account

You will see the list of analytic account.
