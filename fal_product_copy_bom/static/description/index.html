How to use this module

Step 1 : Create a product and Bill of Material, then click Duplicate with BoM
Step 2 : The message "Are you sure you want to duplicate this product with its BoM?" will pop up, and click ok
Step 3 : Product is already duplicated with its Bill of Material
Step 4 : Create a product variant and Bill of Material, then click duplicate with BOM
Step 5 : There is a message "Are you sure want to duplicate this product with its BoM?" click ok
Step 6 : Product variant is already duplicated with its Bill of Material
