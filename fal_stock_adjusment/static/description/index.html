Inventory Adjusment

Refresh Theoritical Qty on On Process Inventory Adjustment

Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : in Odoo, once you begin an inventory adjustment, you have to finish it without taken into account any potential moves during your inventory. In particular, it implies that the warehouse manager cannot prepare the inventory in advance for his team by selecting the required product categories to be checked.

Falinwa behavior : you can refresh the quantity on hand at any moment before the validation which gives flexibility to the warehouse manager to define an inventory process. The adjustment will be done by the difference between the computed quantity and the real quantity at the date you decide (and not at the date you begin the launch the inventory).

Module + : a required flexibility to plan the inventories in your warehouse.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation: 100% of the database with the module Inventory.
  • Recommandation: thanks to this extra feature, you can make your planning of rolling inventory over the month or over the year. upon request, usually when the sales admin is speaking about this recurring issue.

The Features

Inventory Adjusment

Inventory Adjustment or stock opname is to update the quantity product in the system with real quantity on hand.

In Odoo once inventory adjusment is started (in progress), the theoritical quantity (quantity on system), cannot change while other process (Receipt and Delivery Order) is continue. In other way, Inventory Adjustment in Odoo should be done when the stock move is stops.

With this module we are able to refresh the theoritical quantity on in process inventory adjusment.
