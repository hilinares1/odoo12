How To Do

Step 1

Go to Manufacturing > Operations > Manufacturing Orders. Create a manufacturing order
Step 2

Select a product to manufacture, set quantity to produce, then save.
Step 3

After clicking Save, the product on Consumed Materials will be displayed, but still in red. Click Check Availability.
Step 4

Click Check Availability to know the availability of the material, if the product changes to black, it means the product is available. You can see on Miscellaneous tab, Produced Qty is 0 and Remaining Qty is 1. Now, you can click Produce.
Step 5

When you click Produce, it will display Produce wizard, you can set the quantity. Here we set 1 for the quantity. Then click Record Production.
Step 6

After that, the status of the manufacturing order is In Progress and now Produced Qty is 1. If itâ€™s all okay, you can click Mark As Done.
Step 7

Now your manufacturing order is done.

Update Quantity

Step 1

You can update quantity when the status of manufacturing order is in progress. It means there is already produced quantity. Click Update.
Step 2

Set Quantity to produce. Here we set 5 and click Approve.
Step 3

And now we have 4 on Remaining Qty. Click Produce.
Step 4

On Produce Wizard, the quantity is 4, click Record Production.
Step 5

Click Mark As Done, and Produced Qty is now 5.
