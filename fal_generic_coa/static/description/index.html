Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo proposes a very simple generic chart of account which is mainly for testing.

Falinwa behavior : you have access to a full generic chart of account which is easy to read by most of accountant and accurate both with European chart of account (high level of details) and Anglo-saxon CoA (EBITDA approach of the structure).

Module + : this chart of account is based on our 10 years experience as outsourced CFO for international multi-companies.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation : if you do not have a statutory and mandatory chart of account or for your holding to prepare the consolidation, Falinwa recommends to use this chart of account.
  • Recommendation / Best practice : this chart of account is also convenient to use on the holding company in order to define the consolidated ledger account and prepare the consolidation. As it's a mix between French and US approach, it will simplify the matching with a lot of local chart of account.
