Sale Order Delivery and Invoicing Status

This module useful to get status of delivery and invoices of sale orders. Easily filters sale orders with delivered, partial delivered, paid, partially paid.

How it works? Click here to watch video

Key features:
  • Easy to find delivered or partially delivered delivery orders and fully paid or partially paid invoices.
  • Easy to filter delivered sale orders.
  • Easy to filter partial delivered sale orders.
  • Easy to filter fully paid invoices of sale orders.
  • Easy to filter partially paid invoices of sale orders.
