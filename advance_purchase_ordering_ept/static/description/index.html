Advance Purchase Ordering / Reordering

OUT OF STOCK- Nightmare of every business. As a business out of stock can prove to be a nightmare. It not only affects your bottom line, but also your future sales. You need an advanced reordering system in place which can handle reordering and procurement of all your products across all the warehouses. Odoo, by default does not provide facility to place orders automatically based on rules. To overcome this situation, we have developed an Advanced reordering app for Odoo which can handle all automatic purchasing needs of a business.

This app solves 2 underlying problems of a business: 1)What to order? 2)How much to order?

App automatically calculates what to order and how much to order. It even goes a step further and also takes into account procurement rules so that demand is supply is maintained across all warehouses. Don't let your business face the dark side of "Out of stock" with the help of our automatic reordering app for Odoo.

Is this app compatible with Odoo Enterprise as well as Community?

Yes, the app works perfectly well with Odoo Enterprise (On-premise and Odoo.SH) as well as Community. Odoo Online (Cloud) does not allow installation of third-party apps and hence this app cannot be installed on Odoo Online.

Does the reordering or procurement calculation consider the incoming qty?

Yes, this app considers the incoming qty while generating demand in the reordering or procurement calculation.

How does this app manage supplier's Minimum Order Qty rules?

The app will group the demand of all warehouses and compare it with supplier's MOQ. The order will be either based on Supplier's MOQ or Demand, whichever is higher.

What does 'Qty Available to Sale' mean?

Qty Available to Sale means the stock qty that we can actually sell based on the demand or available stock, whichever is lower until the currently placed orders are received.

What does 'Expected Sales' mean?

The app allows you to set Security Days i.e. the next x number of days for which inventory has to be managed in advanced. The Expected Sales field shows the possible sales that can take place during the security days.

What does 'Forecasted Stock' mean?

The app allows you to set Security Days i.e. the next x number of days for which inventory has to be managed in advanced. The Forecasted Stock field shows the possible stock on hand (opening stock) at the beginning of security days.

What is the difference between Reorder Process & Procurement Process ?

Reorder Process is applicable when buying products from the Vendor/Supplier whereas Procurement Process is applicable when procuring stock from another warehouse of the same company or from a different company that belongs to the same owner.

Is it possible to recalculate demand or adjust demand?

Yes, it is possible to recalculate demand as well as adjust sales demand.

How is it possible to automatically create reorder process?

This app contains a report called 'Product Recommendation Report'. This report lists all the products that will be out of stock and based on that, reorder record can be created directly.
