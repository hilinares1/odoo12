Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Odoo proposes a customer rating but not a quantitiative way to lock any new order from a bad debtor.

Falinwa behavior : for bad debtor, whatever the amount of quotation a salesperson can confirm, the manager validation is required.

Module + : prevent to make extra orders on customers who have payment delays.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation : for advanced sales team organization with double signature process.
  • Recommendation / Best practice : fix a default rule for all your customers in order to launch the module. Then you can adjust the rule for each customer if you want to be more strict or more flexible. Falinwa recommends the finance department to do this configuration and fix the default value.

The Features

Multiple Selection Sales Restriction

None

Allowed your customer to buy from us without any restriction

Block

Always block this customer to make transaction with us

Credit Limit

Limit your customer credit. When a customer reach they limit, there will be warning in sales order, and need further approval to continue the transaction.

Invoice Due Age

Whenever this customer has invoice due + N age, there will be warning in sales order if this customer make new transaction.

Credit Limit and Invoice Due Age

Combination of Credit Limit and Invoice Due Age.
