Payment with Provision

Record and Follow up the Payment using Bank Provision

Falinwa Limited

Story behind the features

Odoo behavior : Customer invoices/Vendor bills payment means are Cash or Bank transfer but not financial products.

Falinwa behavior : when you use financial tools like factoring, bank guarantee, letter of credit,..., once you receive the proof of document, you reconcile the related invoices and record them as paid by the financial products. Then, you focus now on the financial product recovery.

Module + : you keep your Account Receivable / Account Payable clean by making the reconciliation at financial product reception and not at cash reception.

Business case : your receive a bank guarantee of 100k$ to pay all your invoice for Customer X. So, all the invoices of Customer X are paid - nothing is no more due - and you have a bank guarantee to be recovered.

Falinwa recommendation :

  • Installation : companies using financial products.
  • Recommendation / Best practice : It's important to record the payment by financial products because the Account receivable/Account payable both include the cash delay (ie due date management) and the risk of non-payment when the financial products only includes the cash flow delay (ie the due date) but no more the risk on unrecovery.

Why We Need This Module

    How to use this module

    Step 1
  • Create a Bank Provision Journal on Accounting > Configuration > Journals
    • Step 2
  • Click Register Payment on Invoices
  • Choose Bank Provision on Payment Method
  • Validate and check invoice state

    • Notes :

    No Bank Provision : Provision Number
    Bank Reconcile : Bank to transfer the provision
    Due Date : Date when provision can be transfered
    Payment Journal : Bank Provision Journal
    Step 3
  • Check your Bank Provision record on Advisor / Bank Provision Menu
    • Step 4
  • Liquid your Bank Provision by click on button Cash
